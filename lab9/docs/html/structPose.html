Detailed Description

template<typename Scalar = double>
struct Pose< Scalar >

Helper class for working with elements of \(\mathsf{SE}(3)\).

Represents a pose as a transformation matrix \(\mathbf{T} \in \mathsf{SE}(3)\):

\[\mathbf{T} = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{R} & \mathbf{r} \\ \mathbf{0}^\mathsf{T} & 1 \end{bmatrix} \in \mathsf{SE}(3) \]

where \(\mathbf{R} \in \mathsf{SO}(3)\) and \(\mathbf{r} \in \mathbb{R}^3\)

Inverse element in \(\mathsf{SE}(3)\).

Computes the inverse of the pose: \((\mathbf{T}^a_b)^{-1} = \mathbf{T}^b_a\)

\[\begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{R}^a_b & \mathbf{r}_{B/A}^a \\ \mathbf{0}^\mathsf{T} & 1 \end{bmatrix}^{-1} = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{R}^b_a & \mathbf{r}_{A/B}^b \\ \mathbf{0}^\mathsf{T} & 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} (\mathbf{R}^a_b)^\mathsf{T} & -(\mathbf{R}^a_b)^\mathsf{T} \mathbf{r}_{B/A}^a \\ \mathbf{0}^\mathsf{T} & 1 \end{bmatrix} \]

Group operation of \(\mathsf{SE}(3)\).

Computes the composition of two poses: \(\mathbf{T}^a_c = \mathbf{T}^a_b \mathbf{T}^b_c\)

\[\begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{R}^a_c & \mathbf{r}_{C/A}^a \\ \mathbf{0}^\mathsf{T} & 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{R}^a_b & \mathbf{r}_{B/A}^a \\ \mathbf{0}^\mathsf{T} & 1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{R}^b_c & \mathbf{r}_{C/B}^b \\ \mathbf{0}^\mathsf{T} & 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{R}^a_b \mathbf{R}^b_c & \mathbf{R}^a_b \mathbf{r}_{C/B}^b + \mathbf{r}_{B/A}^a \\ \mathbf{0}^\mathsf{T} & 1 \end{bmatrix} \]

Vector3 Pose< Scalar >::operator* ( const Vector3 & r) const

Action of \(\mathsf{SE}(3)\) on \(\mathbb{P}^3\).

Transforms a point from one coordinate frame to another. Point alias: \(\mathbf{p}_{P/A}^a = \mathbf{T}^a_b \mathbf{p}_{P/B}^b\)

\[\begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{r}_{P/A}^a \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{R}^a_b & \mathbf{r}_{B/A}^a \\ \mathbf{0}^\mathsf{T} & 1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{r}_{P/B}^b \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{R}^a_b \mathbf{r}_{P/B}^b + \mathbf{r}_{B/A}^a \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \]

Point alibi: \(\mathbf{p}_{B/P}^a = \mathbf{T}^a_b \mathbf{p}_{A/P}^b\)

\[\begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{r}_{B/P}^a \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{R}^a_b & \mathbf{r}_{B/A}^a \\ \mathbf{0}^\mathsf{T} & 1 \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{r}_{A/P}^b \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} \mathbf{R}^a_b \mathbf{r}_{A/P}^b + \mathbf{r}_{B/A}^a \\ 1 \end{bmatrix} \]
